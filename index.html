LeStarZ's
Speak the Beat

A metronome that counts aloud

How to use

Tap the green panel or press spacebar to start and stop.

Time Signature sets how many beats are in each bar. The first beat is always accented.

Subdivision adds quiet in-between clicks — 8th notes or 16th notes — so you can feel the subdivisions without counting them aloud.

Count Style controls what the voice says. All beats counts every beat by number. One only calls the downbeat only — useful at fast tempos. Silent lets the clicks do the work.

Click Tone changes the sound of the click. Try Bell for a chime, Beep for a classic electronic metronome, or Soft for quiet practice.

Tap HERE to Set Tempo — tap the button repeatedly in time and the BPM will lock to your tapping.

The Voice selector lets you choose who counts. Check English only to filter the list.
