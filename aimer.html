LeStar Z's Aimer

THE TABLE: Drag the red (object ball) and white (cue ball) to match your real shot. The app immediately calculates the cut angle and aim point from their positions.

THE DIAGRAM: Eye-level view showing where to aim on the OB. The orange dashed line is your aim point — where the inside edge of the CB must contact the OB. Drag the CB in the diagram to fine-tune, or use arrow keys (1% per press).

SPEED: Faster shots reduce throw. Slower shots increase it.

SIDE: Side english adds throw (OB deflects) and squirt (CB path deflects).

VERT: Follow (top) or draw (bottom). Moves the tip dot on the CB graphic. Does not affect the aim point directly but affects CB path after contact.

DIST: Distance from CB to OB. Squirt from side english accumulates over distance.

BIAS: Your personal correction. If you consistently undercut, slide toward thinner. If you overcut, slide toward fuller.

MAP: Overlay the diamond grid on the table for position reference.
